# Charter

> Durable strategy. Changed only when strategy genuinely shifts (logged in the ledger).
> Aim big, enter narrow. Founded 2026-06-20.

## Destination (the ~3-year dream, as if it already happened)
We became a small, profitable, bootstrapped studio for **owned intelligence** — tools that let
people own their AI's memory and the agents around it instead of renting them. `second-brain`
became the reference implementation for self-owned AI memory (tens of thousands of installs,
the project people cite when they say "don't rent your memory"). `mochu` matured into a paid
loop-engineering offering for teams who want verifiable autonomous improvement on their repos.
A catalog of polished, paid Claude skills throws off recurring revenue that funds the whole thing.
No outside capital. Profitable on our own cash.

## Prime metric
**Monthly net profit.** Until that exists: **first profitable dollar**, then **MRR**.
Everything else (stars, installs, traffic) is a leading indicator, not the score.

## Current wedge (what we take NOW)
**Monetize the skills we already built** by listing **one** of them as a paid product on the
emerging Claude-skill / creator marketplace, and proving someone will pay — while `second-brain`
runs as the *free* credibility flagship (never paywalled).

- **Lead candidate:** `podcast-editor` — crisp, sellable outcome ("messy Chinese recording →
  polished episode, 100% original voice, fillers cut"), a growing 中文播客 audience Stan can reach
  directly via 小红书/即刻, and a result a buyer can feel. (Alt: a "Chinese-creator pack" bundling
  `huoren-lyrics` + `suno-lyrics-generator`.)
- **Why us / why now:** a paid Claude-skill economy is forming *right now* (Gumroad bundles,
  Agent37-style hosted checkout) but most listings are thin wrappers. Stan ships skills with real
  depth (local Whisper + LLM filler-marking + ffmpeg splicing — an actual pipeline, not a prompt).
  First-mover on *quality* in a marketplace full of prompt-dumps.

## Flywheel (what compounds)
Build a differentiated skill → list it as a paid product → revenue + an audience →
that audience installs `second-brain` (free flagship) → stars/credibility →
inbound for `mochu` (B2B) and the next paid skill. Each skill shares the same brand and channel,
so listing #2 is cheaper to sell than #1.

## Operating constraints
- **Budget:** ~$0. Bootstrapped. No paid ads until an organic offer converts.
- **Capacity:** effectively solo (Stan) + autonomous loops (mochu on the product, growth loop on GTM).
- **Tokens/time are the real scarce resource** — cheapest experiment that yields a real signal wins.

## Do-not-do
- **Never paywall `second-brain`'s core.** Its whole pitch is free/local/own-your-data; charging for
  the core kills the wedge. Money from it (if ever) is optional convenience/enterprise add-ons only.
- No deceptive or spammy growth; earn attention honestly.
- `watchtower` stays analysis-only — no individualized financial advice, no trade execution.
- Respect every marketplace's ToS; no misrepresenting what a skill does.
