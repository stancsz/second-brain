## Active

### B1 — Paid skill validation (THE WEDGE) — status: exploring
- **Thesis:** People will pay for a skill that delivers a felt outcome. The cheapest proof is one
  polished skill (`podcast-editor`) listed at a real price with a real checkout.
- **Validation:** A live listing + **≥1 paying customer** OR **≥20 waitlist/interest signups** within
  ~2 weeks of listing. (Listing itself is gated — see approvals.)
- **Runway:** 10 cycles from first listing.
- **Kill criteria:** After the listing is live + 2 promo touches, **zero** clicks/signups → the offer
  or audience is wrong; reposition or switch lead skill.
- **Progress:** 2026-06-20 — founded. Asset exists and is published as a free skill; packaging +
  pricing + channel decisions are the open work. Listing is gated on Stan.
